Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs or drumettes
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Optional: diced onion and/or diced potatoes

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaf, peppercorns, and salt. Add the chicken and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and reserve the marinade. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until browned, about 5 minutes per side.
  3. Add the reserved marinade and any optional ingredients (onion, potatoes) to the pan. Bring to a simmer and cover. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy!
