Kare-kare

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef tripe and/or oxtail
  • 1 cup ground peanuts
  • 1/4 cup annatto seeds
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 cup sliced bok choy or Chinese cabbage
  • 1 cup sliced eggplant
  • 1 cup sliced long beans
  • 1/2 cup sliced banana heart (optional)
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the beef tripe and/or oxtail with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours or until the meat is tender.
  2. Meanwhile, in a separate pan, toast the ground peanuts over medium heat until fragrant. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a separate pan, toast the annatto seeds until fragrant. Remove from heat and grind the seeds into a fine powder. Set aside.
  4. Remove the beef tripe and/or oxtail from the pot and set aside. Reserve the broth.
  5. In the same pot, combine the ground peanuts, annatto powder, and 4 cups of the reserved broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Add the minced garlic and diced onion to the pot. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. Add the sliced bok choy, eggplant, long beans, and banana heart (if using) to the pot. Simmer for 5-10 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Add the cooked beef tripe and/or oxtail to the pot. Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes or until heated through.
  9. Serve the kare-kare over steamed rice and enjoy!
