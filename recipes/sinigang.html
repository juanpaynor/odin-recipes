Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped okra
  • 1 cup chopped taro root (gabi)
  • 1 cup sliced green beans
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce (patis)
  • 1/4 cup tamarind paste (or 1/4 cup tamarind powder mixed with 1/4 cup water)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 cups water
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork belly, tomatoes, onions, okra, taro root, green beans, fish sauce, tamarind paste, salt, pepper, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the pork is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  2. Serve the sinigang over steamed rice and enjoy!
